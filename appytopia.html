Appytopia – The Trailer


Appytopia. Mobile App Draft, publication, video. 2014 - Ongoing. In Glückskartierung: Entwurf eine temporäre Anleitung für Glücksexperimente (Happy mapping: temporary instructions draft for happy experiments)


Appytopia is the draft for a free online mobile application. It helps users organize their temporary “happy locations” in cartographic format. Each happy location is abstracted into an hexagon, a pattern inspired by Walter Christaller’s Central Place Theory. When two or more hexagons come into existance in the same moment (how long is a moment? an algorithm will randomis that), they stick to each other. The hexagon that came together form islands. Appytopia operates as (im)possible travel guide to these islands. It guides its users through and to the happy locations shared by and within its community.
